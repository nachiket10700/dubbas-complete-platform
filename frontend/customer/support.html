Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?
You can track your order in real-time from the "My Orders" section in your dashboard. Click on the active order to see live tracking status.
What is your refund policy?
We offer full refunds for cancelled orders within 5 minutes of placing them. For delivery issues, refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.
How do I change my subscription plan?
Go to "My Subscriptions" in your dashboard, select the plan you want to change, and click "Change Plan". The new plan will be effective from next billing cycle.
Can I schedule deliveries in advance?
Yes! While placing an order, you can select "Schedule for later" and choose your preferred delivery time up to 7 days in advance.
How do I update my payment method?
Go to "Payments" section in your dashboard, click on "Payment Methods", and you can add, remove, or update your payment details.
